General Questions

  • What is BSIT?
    BSIT stands for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. It is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of information technology, including computer systems, software development, networking, and information management.

Curriculum and Courses

  • What are the core courses in a BSIT program?
    The core courses in a BSIT program typically include:
    • Introduction to Information Technology
    • Programming Fundamentals
    • Database Management
    • Computer Networks
    • Systems Analysis and Design
    • Information Security
    • IT Project Management

Tuition and Financial Aid

  • What is the tuition cost for a BSIT program?
    The tuition cost for a BSIT program can vary depending on the institution and location. On average, the cost can range from $20,000 to $50,000 per year for a private university, and $10,000 to $20,000 per year for a public university.
